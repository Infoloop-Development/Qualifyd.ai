Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Qualifyd.ai collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (hashed)
  • Resume Files: PDF or DOCX files you upload for analysis
  • Job Descriptions: Text you paste for resume matching
  • Analysis Results: Scores and recommendations generated from your resume

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide resume analysis and optimization services
  • Store your analysis history for your convenience
  • Improve our services and algorithms
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Processing

Qualifyd.ai uses rule-based analysis to provide transparent and explainable results. Your data is processed securely and is not shared with third parties except as necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Resume files are stored temporarily for analysis and can be deleted at any time through your account.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure password hashing, and secure file storage.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at support@infoloop.co

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
