# Site Information
Site: https://qualifyd.ai
Name: Qualifyd.ai
Description: Resume optimization and ATS analysis tool. Get instant Fit, ATS, and Writing scores with actionable recommendations to optimize your resume for job descriptions.

# Purpose
Purpose: Help job seekers optimize their resumes to match specific job descriptions, pass ATS filters, and increase interview opportunities.

# Key Features
Features:
- Resume analysis against job descriptions
- Three comprehensive scores: Fit Score, ATS Score, Writing Score
- Actionable recommendations for improvement
- Missing skills and keywords detection
- Formatting and writing quality checks
- Optional AI-powered resume rewriting

# Technology
Technology:
- Frontend: React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Analysis: Rule-based deterministic scoring (transparent, explainable)
- Privacy: Rule-based by default, optional AI features
